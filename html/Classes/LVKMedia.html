LVKMedia.m

Overview

The primary domain model of the SDK, representing audio, on-demand video, or a live stream. The canonical source of media metadata and information.

Note, since a media has many potential playback sources (see LVKMediaSource), a media is not by itself playable, but from an end-users' perspective this is what they are selecting. As a convenience, the LVKPlayerController (and LVKPlayerViewController) will auto-select a reasonable default source to play.
