LVKPlayerViewController.m

Overview

A convenience class analagous to MPMoviePlayerViewController capable of being directly inserted into the view stack.

Responsible primarily for initializing an LVKPlayerController instance, hooking the player output to its view, and stopping playback when the view is no longer visible. Good for simple full-screen use cases.

Playback control, events, etc, are accessed via the player property of LVKPlayerViewController.
